Tortellini Soup

Description

This tortellini soup is vegetarian but packs a lot of flavor and you may not even miss the meat. Pair with crusty French bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 zucchini, quartered and sliced
  • 1 package cheese tortellini

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan until shimmering over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions, garlic and tomato ; cook until soft about 5 minutes.
  3. Mix in zucchini and tortellini. Cover and cook until tortellini float on the top and are cooked through, 8 to 10 minutes.
